So What's the Deal?

We are constantly bombarded with think pieces outlining hundreds of ways we need to immediately change our behavior to save the planet.

The result is a tyranny of choice scenario. We're presented with so many options that we don't know where to start.

Let's Fix It.

Loop makes forming environmentally-friendly habits approachable and rewarding with personalized, manageable tasks.

Customizing small steps based on what someone is passionate about leads them to feel more fulfilled by and invested in their actions.

Rewarding completed tasks and tracking progress over time generates feelings of accomplishment and motivates them to keep coming back.

Incorporating community events connects people to the environmental movement around them and lets them see the impact of their actions.

Each task can be easily completed within a day.

Education helps people make informed decisions in the future.

Suggestions lead people towards better habits.
