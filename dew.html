Dew

Brand Positioning / Logo Design / Identity Design

Dew is a rebrand of a San Luis Obispo restaurant and art gallery that highlights all things Central Coast

So What's the Deal?

The previous brand was outdated, inconsistent, and wasn't allowing the restaurant to reach the full potential that comes with being located in the heart of downtown San Luis Obispo.

Let's Fix It.

The new brand should clearly express the philosophy of bringing together the best food, wine, art, and entertainment that the region has to offer.

The new visual language emphasizes the fresh food and friendly, inviting atmosphere, establishing Dew as the go-to spot for Central Coast culture and community.
