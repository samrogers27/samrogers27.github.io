Representing the world of maritime shipping

XVELA Icon Library

I designed a set icons for XVELA, a company that creates cloud-based collaborative tools for the maritime shipping industry.

Skills

Illustration

Iconography

Tools

Adobe Illustrator

XVELA needed custom icons to illustrate complex shipping and software processes and add more personality to the brand.

The linear style of the icons evokes style of XVELA’s logotype and fits seamlessly within their existing identity.

The icons represent XVELA’s values of collaboration, security, and organization, as well as material objects relevant to maritime shipping—think ships, ports, weather, and commodities.
