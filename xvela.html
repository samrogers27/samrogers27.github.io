XVELA Icon Library

Illustration / Iconography

I designed a set of 128 icons for XVELA, a company that creates cloud-based collaborative tools for the maritime shipping industry.

So What's the Deal?

XVELA needed branded icons to use on marketing collateral, presentations, and their newly redesigned website.

Let's Fix It.

The linear style of the custom icon library evokes style of XVELA's logotype and fits seamlessly within their brand.

The icons represent concepts such as XVELA’s values of collaboration, security, and organization, as well as material objects that illustrate aspects of maritime shipping—think ships, navigation, ports, weather, and commodities.
