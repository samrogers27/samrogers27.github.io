So What's the Deal?

The mental health field is fragmented. There is no central means of communication between providers and care networks. This makes it difficult to find updated provider information, make accurate referrals, and collaborate on cases.

Let's Fix It.

Grove provides a referral management, directory, and community platform for mental healthcare providers and case managers. This allows providers to work together in a secure, professional space and connects them to client sources such as hospitals.

Brand

Grove's brand balances the technology aspect of a software startup with the warm, inviting nature of mental health professionals in a memorable and accessible way. The company's goal of bringing different types of providers and healthcare professionals together is expressed in the brand by combining different types of imagery, including patterns, illustration, photography, and collage.
