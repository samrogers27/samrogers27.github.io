Our platform brings mental healthcare providers together in a secure, professional space and connects them to client sources, such as hospitals.

Grow on Grove

Grove’s brand balances the technology aspect of a software startup with the warm, inviting nature of mental health professionals in a fun and accessible way (with plenty of plant puns).

The sentiment of community, support, and success that accompanies the word “Grove” makes it the perfect foundation for a welcoming and compassionate brand.

Truly connected mental healthcare means that everyone has access to the tools they need to do their jobs. For Grove, accessibility was a consideration from the very beginning—from a color palette featuring a variety of WCAG 2.1 AA compliant combinations to a web app that can be used entirely with a screen reader.

A combination of imagery, including patterns, illustration, photography, and collage, visually expresses Grove’s mission to foster collaboration in the mental health industry.
